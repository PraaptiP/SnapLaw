Everything you need to know about SnapLaw

SnapLaw can analyze a wide variety of legal documents including employment contracts, NDAs, service agreements, lease agreements, terms of service, privacy policies, partnership agreements, and more. Our AI is trained on thousands of legal document types to provide accurate analysis across different legal domains.
We take security extremely seriously. Your documents are encrypted during upload and processing, analyzed in a secure environment, and automatically deleted after 24 hours. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) and never store your documents permanently. Our servers are SOC 2 compliant and regularly audited.
Our AI analysis achieves over 95% accuracy in identifying key terms, clauses, and potential issues. However, SnapLaw is designed to assist and inform, not replace professional legal advice. For critical legal decisions, we always recommend consulting with a qualified attorney who can provide personalized legal counsel.
We support PDF, JPG, PNG, and plain text (.txt) files up to 16MB in size. For optimal results, we recommend using PDF or clear image files. Scanned documents with poor image quality may have reduced accuracy.
Most documents are analyzed within 15-30 seconds, regardless of complexity. Larger documents may take up to 2 minutes. You'll see real-time progress updates during the analysis process, and you can view your results immediately when complete.
